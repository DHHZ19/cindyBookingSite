Policy

Dear Guest, for my safety and yours masks are required at time of service. Please do not bring extra guests, only the client being serviced is allowed inside. I do ask my clients to come in with bare nails. I do not offer soak offs or refills on nail sets. Arriving at time of appointment is crutial there will only be a 10 minute grace period. After the 10 minutes the appointment will be cancelled. Deposits are non-refundable. If you need to cancel please ensure you cancel within two business days before the scheduled service date.
